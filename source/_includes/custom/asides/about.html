techno-genous

adjective - \tek-nō-jə-nəs\
Of or pertaining to a process or substance created by human technology; technogenic, anthropogenic, manmade.
Your kids will live in the future.
They’ll need Internet literacy skills and deep knowledge of the digital devices and networks that are already ubiquitous today. They’re not going to learn those things in school. It’s your problem to teach them.
